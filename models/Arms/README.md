## Arm STL Files

| Part | Role | Material | File |
|---|---|---|---|
| Arm1 | Holder of the elbow servo and connection between the arm and the body. | PETG | [Arm1.stl](./Arm1.stl) |
| Arm2 | Connects the gripper with the rest of the hand and provides space for the arm joint connectors. | PETG | [Arm2.stl](./Arm2.stl) |
| ArmGears | Gear set that transfers motion from the servo in Arm1 to Arm2, creating elbow movement. | PETG & ASA | [ArmGears.stl](./ArmGears.stl) |
| ArmGripper | Main hand structure used for opening and closing so the robot can interact with objects. | PETG & TPU| [ArmGripper.stl](./ArmGripper.stl) |
| ArmJointConnectors | Cylindrical connectors that hold the arm structure together and define the elbow motion range. | PETG | [ArmJointConnectors.stl](./ArmJointConnectors.stl) |
| ServoGripperGear | Gear mounted on the gripper servo to enable the opening and closing motion of the gripper. | ASA | [ServoGripperGear.stl](./ServoGripperGear.stl) |
